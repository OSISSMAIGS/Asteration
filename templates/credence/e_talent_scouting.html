About Talent Scouting

Talent Scouting is a weekly activity carried out by SMA Ignatius Global School students, where students get the opportunity to showcase their talents, both in the arts and sports. besides that in this activity there are also several additional sequences such as birthday greetings and the announcement of the winner.

Previous Talent Scouting

Every talent scouting, we always start with a flag ceremony to show appreciation to our country.

After the ceremony, we start our main event with the grandest opening by our masters of ceremony.

We have held a lot of performances over the months, including singing performances...

... dances and flashmob ...

... band and musical instruments performances ...

... and even self defense.

We also occasionally have motivation speeches and other surprises.
